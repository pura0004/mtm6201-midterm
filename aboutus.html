About Us

Our History

The “Au Beau Pain Bakery” is a new bakery that has recently been opened in Downton Ottawa by Valérie and her husband Matthew Fox. Both have been living in France for more than 10 years, working in the French baking industry. And now decided to open a business of their own in Matthew’s hometown. For Valérie baking is an ‘Art’, the concept of her bakery is to bring the French and the Canadian baking culture into one and make the Ottawans discover the specialties of both countries.
